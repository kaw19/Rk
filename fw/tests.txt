This folder contains test sketchs used after Rk assembly.
There are sketchs to test all Rk's elements (sensors and actuators):
1. RGB_LED: verifys LED RGB action, blinking each color for 1 sec.
2. BUZ: beeps with 1 sec. of interval.
3. ULTRA: shows distance measured (cm) in serial monitor.
4. MOT: drives motors with movements forward and backward.
